### Service 1: Analytics ROI Audit

**What it is:**
A structured review of your current analytics initiatives, designed to quantify their financial impact and identify where value is being captured—or lost.

**Who it's for:**
Chief Data Officers and analytics leaders who need to demonstrate ROI to the board, justify existing investments, or make the case for additional funding.

**What you get:**
- Assessment of current analytics initiatives against commercial outcomes
- Measurement framework to capture financial benefits not currently tracked
- Executive-ready report quantifying value delivered and value at risk
- Prioritised recommendations: what to scale, refine, or stop

**Typical duration:** 4–6 weeks

**Outcome:**
Clear, quantified evidence of what your analytics investments are worth—and a roadmap for increasing their impact.

**CTA:** Discuss an Analytics ROI Audit

---

### Service 2: Credit Model Commercialisation

**What it is:**
A focused engagement to translate credit risk model improvements into commercial outcomes—and build the business case for executive approval.

**Who it's for:**
Heads of Credit Risk and Chief Risk Officers at banks, fintechs, and SME lenders who have invested in model development but need to demonstrate financial impact.

**What you get:**
- Model performance review (discrimination, stability, segmentation)
- Commercial impact analysis: avoided losses, revenue uplift, operational efficiency
- Board-ready business case with quantified benefits
- Recommendations for model enhancement or alternative data integration

**Typical duration:** 6–8 weeks

**Outcome:**
A credible, quantified case for your credit model investment—ready for CRO, CFO, or board presentation.

**CTA:** Discuss Credit Model Commercialisation

---

### Service 3: 90-Day Analytics Roadmap

**What it is:**
A strategic planning engagement that delivers a prioritised, fundable analytics roadmap—balancing quick wins with longer-term ML and AI investments.

**Who it's for:**
Senior data leaders who need to align their analytics function with business priorities, secure executive sponsorship, and demonstrate strategic value.

**What you get:**
- Current-state assessment of analytics capabilities and gaps
- Prioritised initiative roadmap with commercial rationale
- Business cases for top-priority investments
- Executive sponsorship strategy and stakeholder mapping
- 90-day implementation plan with milestones

**Typical duration:** 6–8 weeks

**Outcome:**
A clear, funded path forward—with executive buy-in and a plan to demonstrate value within the first quarter.

**CTA:** Discuss a 90-Day Roadmap

## Implementation Notes

**Design principles:**
- White space, clean typography, minimal navigation
- No stock photography of handshakes or skylines
- Consider a single professional headshot on About page
- Mobile-first; many executives will view on phone

**Content priorities:**
- Homepage and Services are primary conversion paths
- About builds credibility for those who want to vet you
- Insights/blog is optional but valuable for SEO over time

**Tracking:**
- Primary conversion: Contact form submission
- Secondary: Time on Services page, scroll depth on Homepage
