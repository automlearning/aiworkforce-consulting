15 years proving what analytics is worth

I've spent my career at the intersection of data, commercial outcomes, and executive decision-making—primarily in banking and financial services.

What I've learned: the technical quality of analytics work rarely determines its impact. What matters is whether it connects to the decisions that drive value.

Most analytics teams are technically capable. Fewer can answer the questions that matter to a CFO, CRO, or board: What will this cost? What will it return? How do we know?

That's the gap I close.

What I bring

Commercial judgement, not just technical skill

I've partnered with Chief Risk Officers, product leaders, and ExCo members to embed analytics into executive scorecards and shape portfolio strategy. I understand what senior stakeholders need to see before they commit funding.

Measurement discipline

I design frameworks that capture financial benefits organisations aren't tracking—giving leaders the evidence to scale, refine, or discontinue initiatives based on outcomes, not assumptions.

Credit risk and customer analytics depth

I've led the development of credit risk models, churn reduction programs, and retention strategies across personal lending, credit cards, and SME portfolios. I know what good looks like in this domain.

Practical AI and ML experience

I'm currently building LLM applications for customer personalisation and next-generation credit scoring. I bridge the gap between AI ambition and commercial application.
