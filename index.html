The measurement gap costs more than the analytics

Most data teams deliver dashboards, models, and insights. Few can answer the question that matters to the board: what is this worth?

Without a clear commercial case, analytics initiatives stall. Funding decisions are based on gut feel. High-value work gets deprioritised while low-impact projects consume resources.

The result: executives lose confidence in data investments, and analytics teams become cost centres instead of strategic assets.

Measurement frameworks that connect analytics to outcomes

I work with Chief Data Officers and senior data leaders to design measurement frameworks that quantify the financial impact of analytics, AI, and ML initiatives.

This isn't about building more dashboards. It's about creating the evidence base that allows executives to make funding decisions with confidence—and gives your team the credibility to shape strategy at the highest level.
