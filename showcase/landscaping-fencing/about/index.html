Our Story

Building Beautiful Outdoor Spaces Since 2014

Landscaping and Fencing Solutions was founded with a simple mission: to help homeowners and businesses in Ballarat create outdoor spaces they love. What started as a small operation has grown into a trusted local business serving the entire Greater Ballarat region.

Led by Tony Musiyiwa, our team brings together years of experience in landscaping, fencing, and property maintenance. We're passionate about what we do, and it shows in every project we complete.

What sets us apart is our commitment to understanding each client's unique needs. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. Instead, we take the time to listen, advise, and deliver customised outcomes that exceed expectations.
